About The Collection

Caroline Henderson's papers consist of letters, articles, and biographical information, as well as many letters written by her daughter, Eleanor. These correspondence primarily document the daily activities and difficulties of the Henderson family and life in the Oklahoma panhandle, including the trials of the Dust Bowl.
